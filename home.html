Hey there, fellow traveler!

My name’s Liz, and I sincerely welcome you to my online world of wanderings and wanderlust. Desk-job worker by day, and adventure pioner by weekend, I created this space with the intent to inspire, educate, and commemorate. First, inspire; because no great adventure starts without the tiniest little glimmer of, “what if?”. Inside each wanderer and adventurer is an insatiable thirst to travel outside the lines. Second, educate; because no great adventure ends without at least a mistake, or two. In turn, these mistakes become lessons. And, what good is learning your lesson if you don’t pass it on?

Finally, to commemorate. I started this page as a way to preserve my memories and reflect on my experiences. While I’d love a reader (or two) to appreciate my work and rack up some travel points, my inspiration to start was personal. However, being an over-sharer, here I am projecting my experiences and exposés onto the world. So, welcome. I hope you stay for a moment, but not for too long, because adventure awaits.

Thank you for reading, and happy travels!
